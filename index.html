Radiaunt Clinical Care Solution Private Limited

Radiaunt Clinical Care Solution Private Limited is healthcare distribution and education company based in Bangalore where we supply a range of medical equipment of international repute. We are specialists in medical equipment for hospitals, clinics and primary care that integrate with the clinicians and clinical services. Our specialist fields include Healthcare consultancy, Medical lmaging, Life Care System devices & Healthcare education.

About

Radiaunt Clinical Care Private Limited

Radiaunt Clinical Care Solution Pvt Ltd is a company focused on AI based Medical Devices analytics and business development training consultants . We network with the leading brands to provide customised patient care solutions with state of the art medical devices & storage & printing solutions to all key corporate hospitals, private hospitals, clinics and to primary care centre in the India region. Radiaunt Clinical Care Solution Private Limited is led by it’s founder & CEO Mr Srinath DN who has 30 years plus Experience in Medical Ultrasound Imaging & Clinical Care solutions working earlier with leading healthcare multinational conglomerates like Wipro GE Healthcare L&T Medical, Dragerwerk AG Germany & Mindray who all have wide presence around the world with their state of the art products .

DN Srinath is also a senior Rotarian & has won major accolades for serving the community service above self motto and has led senior roles as Club President & District Director for Disease Prevention & Treatment in Rotary International District 3192.
